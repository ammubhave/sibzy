Our Story

A group of friends at MIT started Sibzy with a mission to improve the dining experience for vegetarians and vegans by connecting restaurants with customers. Why not have a specialized site that caters specifically to veggie food lovers? To learn more about Sibzy, you can read our FAQ. Join us by creating a profile or by listing your awesome restaurant!

The Sibzy Model

1) Explore specialized restaurant menus of only the vegetarian and vegan dishes - don't worry about an item having meat or broth because it's catered to veggie food lovers!

2) Use our dynamic search feature to search for restaurants based on location, even search for your favorite vegetarian dishes by name.

3) Rate, comment, and upvote your favorite dishes. Give restaurants your feedback to help them improve. See what others recommend.
